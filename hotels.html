Hotels near the venue:

The wedding will be at:

The Mount Without, Upper Church Ln, Bristol BS2 8FN

Hotels include:

  • Premier Inn Bristol City Centre (Lewins Mead) hotel, Lewins Mead, Bristol BS1 2NT
  • Mercure Bristol Grand Hotel, Broad St, Bristol BS1 2EL
  • Hotel du Vin Bristol, The Sugar House, Narrow Lewins Mead, Bristol BS1 2NU

More can be found around Bristol alongside various B&Bs of the Air and traditional varities!

Transport to the venue:

The venue is at the bottom of Saint Michaels hill, so some walking will be involved, there are some steps from the street to the venue entrance.

If you are staying further out than the city centre you catch a variety of buses to the Bus Station, Bristol BS1 3NU, which is just down the hill from The Mount Without.
