💑 The Day

The wedding will be at:

The Mount Without, Upper Church Ln, Bristol BS2 8FN

The ceremony will commence at 12:00 and we will be welcoming guests from 11:30 at the entrance to the Mount Without.

After the ceremony, which should conclude at approximately 13:00, we will move to the garden and crypt for drinks whilst the hall is reconfigured for food.

Day guests are invited at approximately 14:00 to join us for a feast to celebrate our wedding.

After the feast, there will be speeches, readings, and maybe even a song or two!

At 19:00 we invite all of our guests to join us downstairs in the crypt for dancing and drinks!

Pizza will be served from 19:30 for two hours, grab a slice or two at your leisure.

We hope to wind down and retreat to our hotel at midnight and we will say goodbye then!

Please do check out the 🛌 Hotels page for info on getting to the Mount Without if you're coming from further afield.
