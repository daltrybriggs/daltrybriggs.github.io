The Day

The wedding will be at:

The Mount Without, Upper Church Ln, Bristol BS2 8FN

There is step-free access from Trenchard Street to the Church, click here to see how you too can stay on the level (well, there's elevation to be traveresed still, it is Bristol).

- 12:30 -

We will be welcoming guests at the entrance to the Mount Without.

- 13:00 -

The ceremony will commence.

We kindly ask that there is no use of phones or photography during the ceremony.

- 14:00 -

After the ceremony we invite everyone to sign the marriage certificate, and we will then move to the garden and crypt for drinks whilst the hall is reconfigured for food.

- 16:00 -

Day guests are invited to join us for a feast to celebrate our wedding.

The feast will be served on sharing platters and there will be V/Ve/GF options.

- 18:30 -

After the feast, there will be speeches, readings, and maybe even a song or two!

- 19:30 -

Evening Guests arrive and we all head downstairs to the crypt for dancing and drinks!

- 21:00 -

Pizza will be served for two hours, grab a slice or two at your leisure.

- Midnight -

We hope to wind down and retreat to our hotel at midnight and we will say goodbye then!



Please do check out the Hotels page for info on getting to the Mount Without if you're coming from further afield.

Your presence is gift enough, but if you did want to get us something, money for our Honeymoon fund! is enormously appreciated!
